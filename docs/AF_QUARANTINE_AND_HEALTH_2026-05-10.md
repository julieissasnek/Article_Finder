# AF Quarantine And Health Notes

## Why The Quarantine Exists

The 2026-05-10 AF integrity repair detached many misleading paper-to-PDF
attachments. Once those misleading claims were removed, `398` PDFs no longer
had any truthful paper row referencing them. They were therefore moved into a
timestamped quarantine batch rather than deleted.

## What The Quarantined PDFs Are

They are not proven garbage. They are files that became unreferenced after AF
stopped pretending that several different paper rows all shared the same PDF.
Some may be legitimate papers that should later be re-ingested as distinct
records. Others may be redundant copies or low-value residue.

## Operational Rule

Keep them in quarantine with a manifest until one of three actions occurs:

1. re-ingest into AF with a truthful paper row
2. archive as historical residue
3. explicitly dispose after review
